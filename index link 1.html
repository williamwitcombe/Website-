LeBron James

Profile

Lebron Raymone James Sr, was born in Akron Ohio. He was born on the 30th of December 1984 now being 37 years old. He is a American professional basketball player playing for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National basketball Association or the NBA. He has three children Bronny, Bryce and Zhuri James. Bronny is 17 years old. Bryce is 14 years old and Zhuri being the youngest at 7 years old. Lebron's wife Savannah James who he married in 2013 is 35 tears old. He entered the league in 2003 being the number 1 draft pick at the age of 20 years old. He has played in the NBA ever since.
